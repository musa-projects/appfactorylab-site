Privacy Policy

Settla Bills

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Settla Bills does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device. No server, no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking.

Settla Bills ("the App") is developed by AppFactory Lab. This privacy policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

Data You Enter

When you use Settla Bills, you may enter:

  • Bill names (e.g., "Rent", "Netflix")
  • Bill amounts
  • Due dates and recurrence schedules
  • Reminder preferences
  • Optional notes

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No bank account information
  • No credit card numbers
  • No payment processing (bills are tracked, not paid)
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book access
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting data

Push Notifications

Settla Bills uses Apple's local notification system to remind you before bills are due. Notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable them in Settings > Settla Bills > Notifications.

In-App Purchases

Settla Bills offers optional subscriptions (Settla Bills Pro) through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. AppFactory Lab does not receive, store, or access your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-Party Services

Settla Bills does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The App communicates only with Apple's StoreKit framework for purchases.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally. You have full control:

  • Delete individual bills by swiping left
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app

No data persists on any external server after deletion.

Children's Privacy

Settla Bills does not collect data from any user of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If updated, the revised policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Since the App collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
