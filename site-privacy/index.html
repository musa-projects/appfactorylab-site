Website Privacy Policy

appfactorylab.io

Effective date: March 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the website appfactorylab.io ("the Website"), operated by Musa, an independent developer based in Israel, doing business as AppFactory Lab ("I", "me", "my").

This policy covers only the Website itself. Each app has its own privacy policy, accessible from the app's page on this Website.

Information I Collect

Contact form submissions: If you use the contact form on a support page, I receive your name, email address, topic selection, and message. This data is processed by Formspree, Inc. (a third-party form handler) and forwarded to me via email. I use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

I do NOT collect:

  • No cookies (the Website uses zero cookies)
  • No analytics or tracking scripts (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, nothing)
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No account registration or login
  • No automatically collected personal data

Third-Party Services

The Website uses the following third-party services:

  • Vercel (hosting) — may collect standard server logs (IP address, timestamp, page requested) as part of their infrastructure. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree (contact forms) — processes form submissions. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

I do not sell, share, or trade any personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA, the legal basis for processing contact form data is your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) — you voluntarily choose to submit the form. For server logs collected by Vercel, the legal basis is legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — maintaining website security and availability.

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for contact form processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority

To exercise these rights, email me at support@appfactorylab.io.

Your Rights Under CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident:

  • I do not sell personal information
  • You have the right to know what data I collect (see above — only contact form submissions)
  • You have the right to request deletion of your data
  • I do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights

Israeli Privacy Protection Law

I am based in Israel and comply with the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981 (PPL). The Website does not maintain a database of personal information requiring registration with the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (PPA). Contact form submissions are stored only in my email inbox and are not organized as a searchable database.

Israeli users have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of any personal data. Contact me at support@appfactorylab.io.

Children's Privacy

The Website is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through the contact form, please contact me and I will delete it.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in my email for as long as needed to resolve your inquiry, then deleted. I do not maintain a separate database of inquiries. Vercel server logs are retained according to Vercel's data retention policies.

Data Security

The Website is served over HTTPS. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely via Formspree's encrypted infrastructure. I take reasonable measures to protect any personal data I receive.

International Data Transfers

Contact form data may be processed by Formspree (based in the United States) and hosted by Vercel (infrastructure in multiple countries). These services maintain appropriate data protection measures. By submitting a contact form, you acknowledge this transfer.

Do Not Track

The Website does not track users and therefore honors Do Not Track signals by default.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of the State of Israel. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts in Israel.
