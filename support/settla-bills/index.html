Settla Bills

Bill Due Date Tracker for iOS

Frequently Asked Questions
Tap the + button on the Dashboard, enter the bill name, amount, and due date, then choose a recurrence (monthly, quarterly, or yearly). Settla Bills automatically resets the due date after you mark each payment as paid.
When adding or editing a bill, choose how early you want to be reminded — 1 day, 2 days, or 1 week before the due date. Settla Bills uses local notifications on your device. Make sure notifications are enabled in your iPhone's Settings > Settla Bills > Notifications.
All your bill data is stored locally on your device. If you delete Settla Bills, your data is permanently removed. There is no cloud backup or server sync. We recommend marking all bills as paid before uninstalling if you want a record.
The free version supports up to 5 bills. To track unlimited bills, tap the upgrade banner on the Dashboard or go to Settings > Upgrade. Plans are $3.99/month or $29.99/year. You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID subscription settings.
CSV export is available with Settla Bills Pro. Go to Settings > Export Data to download a spreadsheet of all your bills and payment history. The file can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.
